Chapter 7 Bankruptcy in Cincinnati, Ohio

Chapter 7 eliminates most unsecured debts in 3-4 months. Cincinnati residents file at 221 E 4th St in the S.D. Ohio.

What Is Chapter 7?

Eliminates most unsecured debts. A trustee reviews assets, but most Cincinnati filers keep everything. Attend a 341 meeting ~30 days after filing, discharge ~60 days later.

Timeline

  3. Automatic stay stops collections

  5. Debtor education course
  6. Discharge (~60 days after 341)

FAQ

What debts are eliminated?

Credit cards, medical bills, personal loans. Not student loans, taxes, or support.

How long does it take?

3-4 months.
