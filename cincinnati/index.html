Chapter 7 vs. Chapter 13

Chapter 7 - Fresh Start

Eliminates most unsecured debts in 3-4 months. Filing fee: $338.

  • Must pass means test
  • Eliminates credit cards, medical debt
Cincinnati Chapter 7 guide →

Chapter 13 - Payment Plan

Keep property, repay debts over 3-5 years. Filing fee: $313.

  • No means test required
  • Keep home and car

FAQ

Where do I file?

S.D. Ohio. 221 E 4th St, Cincinnati, OH 45202.

How much does it cost?

Ch. 7: $338 + $1,000-$1,800. Ch. 13: $313 + $2,500-$4,000.

Can I keep my house?

$145,425 homestead exemption protects most homes.

Is there free help?

Legal Aid Society of Greater Cincinnati serves qualifying residents.

How long does it take?

Ch. 7: 3-4 months. Ch. 13: 3-5 years.
