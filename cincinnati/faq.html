Bankruptcy FAQ -- Cincinnati, Ohio

Common questions from Cincinnati residents about filing bankruptcy in the Southern District of Ohio.

General

Chapter 7 or Chapter 13?

Ch. 7: eliminates debt, 3-4 months, means test. Ch. 13: 3-5 year plan, no means test. Compare.

Where do I file?

S.D. Ohio. 221 E 4th St, Cincinnati, OH 45202. ohsb.uscourts.gov.

How much does it cost?

Ch. 7: $338 + $1,000-$1,800. Ch. 13: $313 + $2,500-$4,000.

Will it ruin my credit?

Stays 7-10 years but many improve within 1-2 years. Rebuilding credit.

Property

Can I keep my house?

$145,425 homestead. Exemptions.

Can I keep my car?

$4,060 vehicle exemption. Guide.

Retirement accounts?

Fully exempt.

Process

What is the 341 meeting?

Trustee asks about finances, 5-10 min. Guide.

Can I file without an attorney?

Yes. Legal Aid Society of Greater Cincinnati may help.

Can I file again?

Yes, with timing rules. Check eligibility.
