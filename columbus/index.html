Chapter 7 vs. Chapter 13 in Columbus

Chapter 7 - Fresh Start

Eliminates most unsecured debts in 3-4 months. Must pass a means test.

  • Discharge in 3-4 months
  • Filing fee: $338
  • Eliminates credit cards, medical debt
Columbus Chapter 7 guide →

Chapter 13 - Payment Plan

Keep your property while repaying debts over 3-5 years.

  • 3-5 year repayment plan
  • Filing fee: $313
  • Keep your home and car

Cost of Filing in Columbus

  • Chapter 7: $338 filing fee + $1,000-$1,800 attorney
  • Chapter 13: $313 filing fee + $2,500-$4,000 attorney
  • Credit counseling: ~$50 total

Free Legal Help in Columbus

Legal Aid Society of Columbus

The Legal Aid Society of Columbus provides free legal assistance to qualifying low-income residents in Franklin County and surrounding areas.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where do I file in Columbus?

Southern District of Ohio. Courthouse: 85 Marconi Blvd, Columbus, OH 43215.

How much does it cost?

Ch. 7: $338 + $1,000-$1,800 attorney. Ch. 13: $313 + $2,500-$4,000. Details.

Can I keep my house?

Ohio's $145,425 homestead exemption protects most homes. Learn more.

Is there free legal help?

Yes. The Legal Aid Society of Columbus provides free help to qualifying residents.

How long does bankruptcy take?

Chapter 7: 3-4 months. Chapter 13: 3-5 years.
