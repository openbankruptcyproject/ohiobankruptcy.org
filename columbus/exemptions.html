State vs. Federal

Ohio lets you choose. Use state if you have significant home equity. Use federal if you rent and want a larger wildcard.

FAQ

What is the homestead exemption?

$145,425 of equity in your home. Doubles for joint filers.

Can I choose federal exemptions?

Yes. One set or the other, no mixing.

What is the wildcard?

$1,350 applied to any property.
