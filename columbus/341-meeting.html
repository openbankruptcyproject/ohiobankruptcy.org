341 Meeting of Creditors - Columbus, Ohio

The 341 meeting is required in every bankruptcy case. It is brief and less intimidating than most people expect.

What Is It?

A brief hearing ~30 days after filing. A trustee (not a judge) asks questions under oath about your finances. Detailed resource →

Where and When

Location: 85 Marconi Blvd, Columbus, OH 43215 (or phone/video)

Timing: 20-40 days after filing. Duration: 5-10 minutes.

What to Bring

  • Government-issued photo ID
  • Proof of Social Security number
  • Recent pay stubs (60 days)
  • Bank statements
  • Tax returns (most recent)

Common Questions Asked

  • Did you review your petition?
  • Is everything accurate?
  • Did you list all assets and debts?
  • Any property transfers in last 2 years?
  • Any pending lawsuits or expected inheritance?

FAQ

What happens?

Trustee asks about finances, 5-10 minutes. Creditors rarely attend.

Is a judge present?

No. The trustee conducts it. Less formal than court.
