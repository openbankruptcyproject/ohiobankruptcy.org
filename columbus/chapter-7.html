Chapter 7 Bankruptcy in Columbus, Ohio

Chapter 7 eliminates most unsecured debts in 3-4 months. Columbus residents file in the Southern District of Ohio at 85 Marconi Blvd.

What Is Chapter 7?

Chapter 7 eliminates most unsecured debts. A trustee reviews your assets, but most Columbus filers keep everything thanks to Ohio's exemptions.

After filing, you attend a 341 meeting at the S.D. Ohio courthouse. Discharge follows about 60 days later.

The Means Test

To qualify, pass the means test:

  • Income below Ohio median = automatic qualification
  • Income above median = detailed expense calculation
  • Social Security excluded

Debts Discharged vs. Not

Not discharged:

  • Student loans (usually), recent taxes, child support, alimony, fraud debts

FAQ

What debts are eliminated?

Credit cards, medical bills, personal loans, utility bills. Not student loans, recent taxes, or child support.

Do I qualify?

Pass the means test. Below Ohio median = automatic. Details.

How long does it take?

3-4 months from filing to discharge.

Will I lose my car?

Ohio's $4,060 vehicle exemption protects most cars. Learn more.
