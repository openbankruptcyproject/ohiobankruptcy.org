Keeping Your Car in Bankruptcy -- Columbus, Ohio

Most Columbus filers keep their vehicles. Ohio's $4,060 exemption plus reaffirmation and cramdown options protect your car.

Ohio's $4,060 Vehicle Exemption

Equity = car's value minus what you owe. If equity is under $4,060, you keep the car. Add the $1,350 wildcard for extra protection.

Chapter 7 Options

Reaffirmation

Keep paying the loan. Most common.

Redemption

Pay car's current value in a lump sum.

Surrender

Return the car, discharge the debt.

Chapter 13 Cramdown

Car purchased 910+ days before filing: loan reduced to car's value. Interest rate may decrease. Pay through your plan.

FAQ

Can I keep my car?

Usually yes. $4,060 exemption covers most situations.

What is cramdown?

Chapter 13 reduces loan to car's value if purchased 910+ days ago.
