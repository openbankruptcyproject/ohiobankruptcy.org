Chapter 13 Bankruptcy in Columbus, Ohio

Chapter 13 lets you keep property while repaying debts over 3-5 years. Columbus residents file at 85 Marconi Blvd in the S.D. Ohio.

What Is Chapter 13?

You propose a repayment plan to pay back debts over 3-5 years. A trustee distributes your monthly payments to creditors. Powerful for:

  • Stopping foreclosure and catching up on mortgage
  • Preventing car repossession
  • Paying back taxes over time
  • Protecting co-signers

Plan Length

  • Below median: 3 years
  • Above median: 5 years

Saving Your Home

The automatic stay stops foreclosure immediately. Catch up on missed payments over 3-5 years. Ohio's $145,425 homestead exemption protects your equity.

FAQ

How does Chapter 13 work?

3-5 year repayment plan. Trustee distributes your payments. You keep all property.

Can I save my house?

Yes. Automatic stay stops foreclosure. Catch up through your plan. Details.

How long is the plan?

3 years (below median) or 5 years (above median).
