Bankruptcy FAQ -- Columbus, Ohio

Answers to common questions from Columbus residents about filing bankruptcy in the Southern District of Ohio.

General

Chapter 7 or Chapter 13?

Ch. 7 eliminates debt in 3-4 months (means test required). Ch. 13 is a 3-5 year plan. Compare.

Where do I file?

S.D. Ohio. 85 Marconi Blvd, Columbus, OH 43215. ohsb.uscourts.gov.

How much does it cost?

Ch. 7: $338 + $1,000-$1,800. Ch. 13: $313 + $2,500-$4,000. Details.

Will bankruptcy ruin my credit?

Stays 7-10 years on report but many see improvement within 1-2 years. Rebuilding credit.

Property

Can I keep my house?

$145,425 homestead exemption. Guide.

Can I keep my car?

$4,060 vehicle exemption. Guide.

Retirement accounts?

Fully exempt. Cannot be taken.

Process

What is the 341 meeting?

Trustee asks about finances, 5-10 minutes. Guide.

Can I file without an attorney?

Yes. Legal Aid Society of Columbus may offer free help.

Can I file again?

Yes, with timing rules. Check eligibility.
