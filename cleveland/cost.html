How Much Does Bankruptcy Cost in Cleveland?

A complete breakdown of bankruptcy costs for Cleveland residents - filing fees, attorney fees, required courses, and options if you cannot afford to pay.

Filing Fees

ChapterFiling FeeNotes
Chapter 7$338Can request installments or fee waiver
Chapter 13$313Can be paid through your plan

Attorney Fees in Cleveland

ChapterTypical Range
Chapter 7$1,000 - $1,800
Chapter 13$2,500 - $4,000

Chapter 13 attorney fees can typically be paid through your repayment plan, so you may only need a small amount upfront.

Other Required Costs

  • Pre-filing credit counseling: $15-25
  • Post-filing debtor education: $15-25
  • Credit report: Free from annualcreditreport.com

Fee Waivers and Assistance

Fee waiver: Chapter 7 filers with income below 150% of federal poverty guidelines can apply to have the filing fee waived.

Installments: Pay in up to four installments over 120 days.

Free legal help: The Legal Aid Society of Cleveland provides free assistance to qualifying low-income residents.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does Chapter 7 cost in Cleveland?

Filing fee: $338. Attorney: $1,000-$1,800. Credit counseling: ~$50 total. Typical total: $1,400-$2,200.

How much does Chapter 13 cost?

Filing fee: $313. Attorney: $2,500-$4,000 (payable through your plan).

Can I get the filing fee waived?

Yes, if income is below 150% of poverty guidelines. You can also pay in installments.

Can I file without an attorney?

Yes, but mistakes can be costly. Contact the Legal Aid Society of Cleveland for free help.
